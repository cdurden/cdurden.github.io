Learning objectives for test 1

  1. Given a verbal description of the pattern of change of a quantity over discrete time steps (such as simple or compound interest), represent the pattern of change by writing a difference equation.
  2. Given a difference equation and an initial condition, find the terms a) iteratively and b) using the general solution.
  3. Given a difference equation and an initial condition, describe qualitatively the behavior of the solution.
  4. Given information about a savings account or loan (interest rate, period of compounding) and a savings goal or a loan repayment goal, devise a savings or payment plan to meet the goal.
  5. Explain what the value of the derivative function at a given value of the independent variable says about the graph.
  6. Find the derivative of a power function (using the power rule).
